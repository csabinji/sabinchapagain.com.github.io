About Me

About Me

Sabin Chapagain (born October 25, 1998), is a Nepalese rapper, songwriter and beat producer. He was born in Damak, Jhapa. He started making music in 2015 with his debut mixtape "Sangarsha". He moved to Kathmandu in 2018 to attend his college and continue to make Hiphop music. He grew up listening to Yama Buddha, Mc flo and Girish Khatiwada.


He recently started his own recording studio where any new talent is welcomed.
